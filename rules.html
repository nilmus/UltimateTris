The Rules

What is Ultimate Tris?

Ultimate Tris is the evolution of Tic-Tac-Toe: you play on a wider board, and you score higher points the longer your streaks

Point System

  • 3 in a row - 1 point
  • 4 in a row - 3 points
  • 5 in a row - 7 points
  • 6 in a row - 15 points
  • 7 in a row - instant win!
